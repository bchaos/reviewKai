Getting started

Add games to your library

Using the library tab at the top add a few games you liked and disliked to your library. You can include thoughts to allow others to get a better idea of what games to get or avoid

Library

Matching you

Review Kai will magically match you with Pro reviewers and your peers and will create a library of personal recomendations. As you add more games to your library the better the pros and peers can understand you and the better recomendations they will give.

You can also find and add confidants. Gamers who opinons your trust over everything else their opinions will be weighted more highly in our calculations. You can even search their libraries to see what they think before you add them.
