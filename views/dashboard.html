Getting started

Add games to your library

Using the libarary tab at the top add a few games you liked and disliked to your library. You can include reviews to allow others to get a better idea of what games to get or avoid

Matching you

Review Kai will magically match you with Pro reviewers and your peers and will create a library of personal Recomendations

Searching for a game

You can also search for a game using the search bar at the top or below

Continuously updating

As you add more games to your library the better the pros and peers can understand you and the better recomendations they will give.
